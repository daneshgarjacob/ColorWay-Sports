The Athletics are playing in West Sacramento while the Las Vegas stadium is being built, and the team store at Sutter Health Park is leaning into it. Sacramento-branded hats and gear are on the shelves, and a new gold "Sacramento" alternate jersey debuts tomorrow for "Sacramento Saturdays." Here's what the full A's identity looks like right now.

## The Sacramento Gold Alternate Jersey

The gold alternate jersey with "Sacramento" in green script across the chest is a strong look. The gold base with green lettering and white piping is clean, and the color combination pops on the field. It's still a little odd seeing "Sacramento" on an Athletics jersey, but that's just going to take some getting used to. For the fans in Sacramento, this is exciting, and we're here for it.

The jersey will be worn every Saturday home game, and every fan at the April 4 game against the Astros gets a replica.

## The On-Field Look

<img src="/images/posts/athletics-sacramento-merch-2026/lineup-las-vegas-patch.jpg" alt="Athletics 2026 jersey with Las Vegas sleeve patch starting lineup" style="width: 100%; border-radius: 10px;" />

The regular game jerseys just say "Athletics" across the chest with no city name. The Las Vegas logo patch has been on the sleeve since last season, so the team is already wearing Vegas branding on the field while playing in Sacramento. The other sleeve has the 125th anniversary patch with the elephant logo and every city the franchise has called home: PHI, KC, OAK, and now SAC.

Owning the Sacramento identity right now is the smart move. These fans are the ones actually showing up. It'll be interesting to see what the full Las Vegas Athletics brand looks like once the stadium gets closer to opening.

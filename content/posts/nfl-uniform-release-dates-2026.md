It's uniform season in the NFL. Multiple teams have confirmed new jersey reveals coming over the next few weeks. Here's what we know so far.

## Atlanta Falcons - April 2 at 10:00 AM ET

The Falcons confirmed that their new uniforms are dropping tomorrow morning. We already got a look at what appears to be the leaked red jersey, and if the full set matches that direction, we're excited. Clean numbers, same logo, and a return to simplicity after years of a set that tried too hard.

## Washington Commanders - April 15

The Commanders announced that their new uniforms are coming on April 15. This is the one we've been waiting for. Washington has been through a lot of identity changes over the past few years, and this feels like the chance to finally get it right.

<div style="display: flex; justify-content: center; margin: 0 0 2em;">

</div>

We're hoping they lean into the throwback look that they wore as their alternate this past season. That set was easily the best thing they've put on the field in years. The burgundy and gold with the classic striping and the old-school feel. If the new primary set looks anything like that, Washington is going to have one of the best uniforms in the league.
